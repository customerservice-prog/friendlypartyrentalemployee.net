Delivery Driver Hiring Test

Friendly Party Rental — delivery & warehouse-to-site role. Please answer every question in your own words, then submit the form at the bottom.

Section 1: Company knowledge

Use what you know about our business from the website, training materials, or instructions you were given.

Q1. What is the name of our company, and what city are we physically based in? (Not just "Syracuse" — what specific town?)

Q2. What is our main phone number that customers should call for pricing, availability, or bookings?

Q3. What is our customer service email address?

Q4. What is our street address?

Q5. Before you leave the yard on a delivery, what do you check on the vehicle and how do you confirm cargo is safe for the road?

Section 2: Judgment on the route

Describe what you would say or do in each situation.

Q11. Construction adds 45 minutes to your route and the customer's setup window is tight. What do you do?

Q12. A customer is angry at the curb and says: "You're way late — I want a full refund right now, in cash." How do you respond?

Q13. The customer claims some items on the truck are wrong or were not ordered and asks you to take them back immediately. What do you do?

Q14. You're moving a heavy cart up a steep driveway and you're working mostly alone. How do you handle it safely?

Q15. What are THREE safety priorities you keep in mind on every delivery?

Open-ended questions — give a clear, professional response.

Q18. Why does professional appearance (uniform, hygiene, calm tone) matter when you're on a customer's property?
