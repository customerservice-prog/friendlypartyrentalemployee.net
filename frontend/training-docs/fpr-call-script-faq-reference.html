❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How far in advance should I book?
As soon as possible — especially for peak summer months and weekends. We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance to ensure availability.
Do you deliver and set up?
Yes! We deliver, set up, and pick up all equipment. You don't have to lift a finger.
Do you do same-day delivery and pickup?
Yes — we offer same-day drop off and pickup on weekends, which is ideal for venues that have back-to-back bookings.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. Please contact us at 315-884-1498 or by email as soon as possible.
Is a deposit required?
Yes, a deposit is required to confirm and secure your booking date. Full details are provided during checkout.
How do I know what's available on my date?
Go to friendlypartyrental.com, click "Order By Date," enter your event date, and only available items will be shown.
Do you have package deals?
Yes! We have package deals that bundle popular items at a great value. Visit the "Package Deals" category online to see current options.
Is there a promo or discount available?
Yes — use promo code SAVE20 at online checkout for a discount. This is a limited-time offer.
Do you rent bounce houses and water slides?
Absolutely! We carry a selection of bounce houses and waterslides. Check availability for your date online.
What payment methods do you accept?
For detailed payment questions, direct the customer to call 315-884-1498 or complete their order online.

🚨 When to Escalate to Nicole / Management

Escalate immediately if customer:

  • Is asking about a specific item's price and you can't find it
  • Had a delivery problem, damage, or missing item
  • Wants to negotiate pricing or a custom quote
  • Has a complaint that you can't resolve
  • Is asking about an order that's already confirmed
  • Needs same-day service or urgent accommodation
  • Has a question about tent permits or large event setup
